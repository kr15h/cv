Krisjanis Rijnieks

New Media Designer and Artist

Krisjanis Rijnieks is a new media designer and artist who uses programming, projection mapping and digital fabrication as his main means of expression. The outcome of his work ranges from web applications, interactive and projection mapping installations to digital fabrication artifacts and graphic design.
